This Lady Cowhorse from Bob's Custom Saddles is brand new — never ridden. The tooling pattern is a distinctive star/cross/cathedral design that sets this build apart from standard basketweave or floral models. Rich mahogany leather covers the swell, skirt, and fender panels, accented by square silver sunburst conchos at each dee.

The black elephant-print seat insert adds bold contrast and functional grip — exactly what a cow horse rider needs during fence work. At 15½ inches, this is a true lady's fit, purpose-built for the female NRCHA competitor who wants a new saddle at a below-retail price. Contact David to discuss tree width fit for your horse.
