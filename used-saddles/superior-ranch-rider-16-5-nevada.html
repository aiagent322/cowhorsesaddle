This AM Superior Saddlery build pairs the signature #1 flower tooling with a classic Nevada border — the decorative rope-twist border that frames the skirt and jockey panels and gives the saddle a structured, layered look distinct from Superior's plain floral or basketweave builds. The dark espresso leather reads richer and deeper than their standard mahogany, giving this saddle a distinctive presence in any barn.

The elephant-print seat insert provides excellent grip and durability through long competition days. Scalloped round silver conchos accent each dee. AM Superior Saddlery hang tag still attached. At 16½ inches with the darker leather palette, this is an excellent fit for the rider who wants a large-seat Superior build with a more understated, classic look.
